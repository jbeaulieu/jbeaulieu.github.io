MIT

2012 - 2017. S.B., Electrical Engineering and Computer Science

Here you will find relevant coursework I have completed during my stay at MIT. For most classes, clicking on the subject number will take you to that course's official subject listing on the MIT's Registrar's webpage. Note that course numbers and offerings change from year to year - I endeavor to maintain as current a listing as possible here.

Electrical Engineering & Computer Science

  • 6.01 - Introduction to EECS: Robotics

    • System modeling behavior through sensor-based control systems. Probabilistic inference solutions. Circuit and transducer design.

  • 6.02 - Introduction to EECS: Communications Networks

    • Information Entropy, data compression algorithms, error-corrective encoding schemes. Signal processing including interference, filtering, modulation, and multiplexing. Packet switching and queueing principles, routing protocols, and data transport protocols.
