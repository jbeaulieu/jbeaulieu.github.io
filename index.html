MIT

Computer Science has been my chief passion since I was young; I was incredibly fortunate to have the chance to pursue it at MIT. Through my studies, I became interested in the Internet of Things, internet policy, and mobile app development. In June of 2017, I graduated with a degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.

Learn more

Regal

I currently work at Regal Beloit Corp., a designer and manufacturer of electric motors. We create everything from air-conditioning motors to industrial pumps for offshore oil wells. I am part of a rotational leadership development team, and my current projects involve designing software for Regal's Internet of Things initiative, making our products smarter and easier to use.
