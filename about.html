About Me

Welcome!

Hi, I'm Jon! I'm a software developer with interests in everything from roadtrips to hobby electronics. I've set up shop in this corner of the internet to showcase some of my background, and to have a central hub for all of my projects and professional connections. Have a look around, and get inspired!

My Work

Professionally, my focus is within the realms of mobile devlopment and web applications. I enjoy making accessible software that can be used by anyone, whether it directly improves their quality-of-life or simply provides an element of convenience. I have an eye for user-centered design, and I'm just as comfortable meeting with end users as I am coding server backends.

My Projects


I’m not inclined to make grand pronouncements about the future of software, but if anything kills off commercial software, let me tell you, it won’t be open source software. They needn’t bother. Commercial software will gleefully strangle itself to death on its own licensing terms.
-Jeff Atwood

I am a staunch believer in the principles of open-source software. I believe that the core purpose of software is to improve the lives of its users, and that those improvements are best implemented when people are free to collaborate or work off of each other's ideas. To that end, the majority of my software projects are provided under the MIT license, giving you free reign to use or change them as you see fit - please just remember to attribute me as the original creator, so that others can find my work as well!

Head over to my Projects page to see a list of my past creations and some of the stuff I'm working on right now, or check out my Google Play listing to view and download my published Android apps. You'll never see ads or unnecessary permissions built into any of my mobile apps - I take pride in the fact that my projects are provided out of a desire to help and inspire other creators like me, rather than to make some ad-based revenue.
